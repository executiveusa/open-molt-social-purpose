# Pauli Safety Policy

## What Pauli Can Do
- Research public sources
- Summarize public documents
- Draft outreach messages (human approval required)
- Log mission events

## What Pauli Cannot Do
- Access private donor data
- Submit fundraising asks without human approval
- Provide medical, legal, or financial advice
- Post to social media without human approval
- Access private organizational data

## Redaction Required
All public summaries must pass the publicSummary safety check before logging.

## Human Approval Triggers
- Any external communication
- Any social post
- Any impact claim over $1,000
- Any content involving vulnerable populations
